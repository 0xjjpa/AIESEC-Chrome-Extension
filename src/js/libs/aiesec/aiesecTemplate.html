AIESEC Chrome Extension

A simple set of tools to improve the experience on MyAIESEC.net

  • TN Search Tool: Usually looking for TN's can be a time consuming task. Through this tool, you can look for TN's in a faster way, store your common searches and save the TN's you like the most.

  • TN Monitor: After saving your TN's in the TN Search Tool, you probably want to see all its details. The monitor helps you view your favorite TN's in a clean a manageable way.
